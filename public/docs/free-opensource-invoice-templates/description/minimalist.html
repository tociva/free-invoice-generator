Minimalist

The Minimalist Invoice Template embodies clean design and thoughtful structure, making it a great choice for freelancers, small businesses, and professionals who want a modern, visually pleasing invoice without unnecessary clutter. The layout is structured with two main sections: the main content area and a slim right accent bar. The right side uses a stylish vertical gradient strip with shades of teal and cyan (#7FCDCD to #5FB3B3), serving as a subtle but distinct design highlight that elevates the overall aesthetic.
