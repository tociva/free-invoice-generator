Modern Yellow Accent Invoice Template


This modern invoice template blends professional design with a bold yellow accent, making it both eye-catching and practical for businesses across industries. Built using the clean and contemporary Inter font, the layout ensures clear communication of billing details while maintaining a sleek aesthetic. The header section prominently features the company logo and sender details in a yellow background, establishing a vibrant brand identity right at the top. On the opposite side, recipient details, invoice number, and payment dates are organized cleanly for easy referencing. The invoice table is designed for clarity, with alternating item rows and right-aligned numerical values for better readability. It supports tax breakdowns such as CGST and SGST, making it ideal for businesses in regions with dual taxation systems. The totals section calculates item total, discounts, subtotal, tax, and grand total, with the final amount emphasized in a highlighted box. This section ensures that your clients never miss the most critical billing info. The footer section includes the preferred payment method and space for terms or additional notes. Responsive design principles ensure that the layout adjusts gracefully on mobile screens, maintaining usability on all devices.
