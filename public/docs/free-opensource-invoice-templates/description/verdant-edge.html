The Elegant Green Accent Invoice Template delivers a refined and professional layout, ideal for modern businesses, freelancers, and agencies looking for a polished invoicing solution. With a visually appealing combination of deep green and minty teal accents, this template enhances branding while maintaining clarity and functionality. The structure begins with a clean header displaying the invoice title and company logo. Following this is a well-organized three-column billing section, providing complete information about the sender, recipient, and invoice details using customizable placeholders like [[org_name]], [[customer_email]], and [[invoice_number]]. A detailed itemized table supports GST calculations, including CGST and SGST columns for each line item, ensuring compliance for businesses operating in tax-sensitive regions. The summary box at the bottom neatly presents item total, discount, subtotal, tax, round-off, and a bold grand total using [[grand_total]].
