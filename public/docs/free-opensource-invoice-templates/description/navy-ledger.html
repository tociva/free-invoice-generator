Navy Ledger

This professional invoice template features a clean, corporate design with a bold blue accent, making it ideal for modern businesses seeking a refined and organized billing layout. Built with responsiveness in mind, the template includes structured sections for sender and recipient details, invoice metadata, itemized billing, tax breakdown (CGST & SGST), and a payment summary. The prominent header with a colored background ensures brand visibility, while the use of Google Fonts and CSS variables ensures visual consistency and customization flexibility. The layout also supports placeholders such as [[org_name]], [[customer_name]], [[item_name]], and [[grand_total]], allowing seamless integration into dynamic web applications. Whether used by SaaS companies, consultants, or service-based businesses, this invoice template is print-ready, mobile-optimized, and designed to make a professional first impression on clients while simplifying financial documentation.
