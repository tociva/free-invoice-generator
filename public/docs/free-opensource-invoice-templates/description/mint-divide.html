Mint Divide

The Green Invoice Template is a clean, elegant, and eco-inspired HTML invoice layout perfect for freelancers, agencies, consultants, and small businesses looking to present their billing in a stylish and professional format. Featuring a soothing green and white gradient header, this template instantly communicates trust, calmness, and financial clarity. Designed with a responsive layout and printable format in mind, this template organizes all essential invoicing information including company details, billing information, line items, and totals, with precise alignment and excellent use of whitespace. It uses semantic HTML tags, accessible font choices (Segoe UI), and modern CSS styles to ensure optimal rendering across all devices and screen sizes.
