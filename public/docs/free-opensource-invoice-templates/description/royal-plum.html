Royal Plum

Modern Violet-Themed Invoice Template with GST Breakdown – Responsive & Professional Design


This professional invoice template features a sleek violet-themed aesthetic, offering a modern and clean layout perfect for businesses of all sizes. Built using HTML and CSS, the design prioritizes readability, usability, and responsiveness—ensuring optimal display across both desktop and mobile devices. At the top, the template includes a stylish header with company branding and logo placement. It is followed by a prominently styled contact information box and a well-organized section for customer billing details and invoice metadata, such as invoice number, issue date, and due date. The centerpiece of this template is its detailed itemized table, which supports placeholders for item name, quantity, unit price, CGST, SGST, and the calculated total. This makes it ideal for Indian businesses or any operation that needs tax component visibility in invoices. Each row is cleanly styled with alternating backgrounds for easy viewing. Financial summary sections such as item total, discount, subtotal, tax amount, round-off, and grand total are neatly aligned to the right in a dedicated summary area. The total is highlighted within a violet pill-shaped banner to draw attention. The invoice concludes with a payment information footer, including bank details, making it easy for clients to process payments. Additionally, the responsive design ensures that all sections reflow gracefully on smaller screens, maintaining a professional appearance. This invoice template is fully customizable with placeholder tags (like [[item_name]], [[org_name]], etc.) to allow easy integration with your backend system or invoice generation tool. Ideal for: Freelancers, SMEs, service providers, and businesses that require GST-compliant invoices with a modern look and responsive format.
