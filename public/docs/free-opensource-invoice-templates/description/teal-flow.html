Elegant Teal Invoice Template – Stylish, Structured & Ready for Business


The Elegant Teal Invoice Template brings a modern, calming teal aesthetic to professional invoicing. With a sharp design powered by the Roboto font and soothing shades of #009688 and #00695c, this invoice layout is perfect for freelancers, startups, and corporate teams aiming for clarity, structure, and visual polish. The layout balances branding and billing details through clearly defined sections—featuring a logo, sender and recipient information, detailed tax-calculated itemization (including CGST and SGST), and an intuitive totals breakdown. This invoice is also print-friendly, responsive, and built with semantic, customizable HTML.
