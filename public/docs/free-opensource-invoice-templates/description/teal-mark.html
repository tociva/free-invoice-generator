Modern Business Invoice Template – Professional & Polished Design


The Modern Business Invoice Template – Steve Cayton Edition is a sleek and professional invoice layout ideal for consultants, freelancers, agencies, and service providers. Designed with a stylish dark teal palette (#2b6f6e) and structured sectional layout, this template ensures your billing looks clean, authoritative, and brand-aligned. It features a responsive and printable format with fully customizable placeholders such as [[org_name]], [[invoice_number]], and [[item_price]], making it easy to integrate with backend systems or invoicing software.
