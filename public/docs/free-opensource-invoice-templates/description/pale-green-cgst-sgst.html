Pale Green CGST-SGST

This Modern Green Invoice Template blends simplicity with elegance, making it ideal for freelancers, small businesses, startups, and agencies looking to present a fresh and organized invoice layout. With its clean white background, soft green accents (#98FB98), and a circular logo header, the template reflects clarity, eco-friendliness, and professionalism. Designed for optimal readability, it includes detailed customer and invoice information, a GST-compliant item table (with columns for CGST and SGST), and a structured total breakdown. The right-aligned numerical columns enhance scannability, while hover effects in the item table add a modern touch to printed or PDF versions.
