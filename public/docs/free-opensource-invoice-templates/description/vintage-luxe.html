Elegant Brown Gradient Invoice Template – Garamond A4 Print-Ready Design


This stylish and professional invoice template features a rich brown-to-copper gradient header, bringing a modern yet refined aesthetic to your billing documents. Ideal for creative agencies, consultants, or boutique businesses, this template balances sophistication with clarity using the classic Garamond serif font and a minimal layout on A4 paper. The header section showcases your brand logo and a prominent “INVOICE” title, set against a warm gradient background that adds visual distinction. The client and invoice details are neatly organized into two columns, with billing information on the left and invoice metadata (number, issue date, due date) on the right—all presented on a light gray background for readability. An itemized billing table follows, presenting products or services with columns for quantity, unit price, and total cost. Subtle borders and spacing maintain clarity without visual clutter. The summary section aligns totals (including discounts, subtotals, and rounding adjustments) to the right, with the grand total highlighted in bold. The footer is split into two sections: one for payment information (bank name, account name, and account number), and the other for organization contact details. A centered “THANK YOU!” note at the bottom adds a courteous closing touch. Optimized for print and digital delivery, this invoice template is both aesthetically pleasing and functional. It’s perfect for businesses that want to maintain a premium image while ensuring clear, structured communication with clients.
