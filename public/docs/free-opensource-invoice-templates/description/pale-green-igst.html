Pale Green IGST

This Professional IGST Invoice Template offers a modern, polished layout with a clean white background and soothing green highlights (#98FB98). Designed for businesses operating across state lines in India, this invoice format includes an IGST column, making it fully compliant with Integrated Goods and Services Tax (IGST) regulations. The template presents company branding with a circular logo header, followed by clearly separated sections for sender (issuer) and billing information. Each itemized entry includes quantity, price, IGST, and calculated totals. A dedicated totals block helps customers quickly understand the breakdown of the invoice amount.
